About Us

The Story Behind D-Tales

Welcome to D-Tales, where art meets imagination. Founded by Deeksha Dwivedi, our gallery showcases a unique collection of contemporary artworks that tell stories through colors, textures, and emotions.

Each piece in our collection is carefully crafted to create an immersive experience, inviting viewers to explore the depths of artistic expression and creativity.

Our mission is to bring exceptional art to art lovers and collectors worldwide, while supporting and promoting the vision of our talented artist.
